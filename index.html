why downroot?

By the year 2030, we might only have 10% of Rainforests left and it can all disappear in a hundred years.

70% of deforestation is linked to production of agricultural goods consumed around the world

Regardless of "zero-deforestation" commitments, supply-chain scandals continue to arise

OUR MISSION

We aim to help businesses root-down their environmental responsibility through creating a universal commitment: the downroot pledge. We are a resource for businesses as they strive to achieve sustainable supply chain management and overall CSR. Downroot is bringing everyone one step closer to a net zero deforested world.

What We Do

Downroot has three main strategies to root environmental responsibility in business: the pledge, hands-on activities, and fundraising initiatives. An individual or a company can live by the downroot values, but our goal is to get companies giving back and getting active to represent corporate responsibility.

Click an icon to the left to learn more.

Pledges

The downroot pledge requires the pledgees follow guidelines for sustainability and must meet a benchmark for giving back. By pledging, businesses not only claim responsibility for the harmful effects they have on the environment, but also implement a mindset focused on better operations and company culture.

Events

Volunteering can be beneficial for both the environment, team-building tactic, and employee morale. Give back and strengthen your company culture all in one go. Downroot will organize it for you that best fits your needs. Ocean clean-ups, gardening, re-forestation, fundraiser planning, and more! Need help with implementing your corporate social responsibility in general? Check out our CSR on-site CrashCourse.

reforestation

All proceeds Downroot receives goes toward operating the organization and planting trees in order to achieve a net zero reforestation world. Whether hired by businesses or planting on our own time, Downroot is determined to reverse the effects of big business on the environment.
