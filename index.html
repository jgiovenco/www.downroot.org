OUR MISSION

We aim to root environmental responsibility within businesses through implemeting a standard of measurement and volunteering program. We serve as a resource for companies in an attempt to strengthen their relationship with their audience and the earth.

What We Do

Downroot has three main strategies to root environmental responsibility in business: the pledge, hands-on activities, and fundraising initiatives. An individual or a company can live by the downroot values, but our goal is to get companies giving back and getting active to represent corporate responsibility.

Click an icon to the left to learn more.

Pledges

The downroot pledge requires the pledgees follow guidelines for sustainability, must incorporate volunteering, and must meet a benchmark for giving back. By pledging, businesses not only claim responsibility for the harmful effects they have on the environment, but also implement a mindset focused on 'healing'.

Events

Volunteering can be beneficial for both the environment, team-building tactic, and employee morale. Give back and strengthen your company culture all in one go. Downroot will organize it for you that best fits your needs. Ocean clean-ups, gardening, re-forestation, fundraiser planning, and more! Need help with implementing your corporate social responsibility in general? Check out our CSR on-site CrashCourse.

Movements

All proceeds Downroot receives goes toward operating the organization and helping areas specifically harmed by business activity. Movements include fundraising to help areas affected by oil spills or palm-oil deforestation to name a few. Check out the current fundraising initiatives Downroot is focusing on.
