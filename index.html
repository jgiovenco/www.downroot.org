downroot

A 501C3 non-profit based in New York

we reverse the environmental effects of business and demand corporate responsibility

why downroot?

because the nature of today's business shouldn't be at the expense of the nature of our planet.

Top firms cause nearly $2.2tn of environmental damage from air pollution.

Companies receive subsidies for fossil fuels which allow them to produce cheap plastic that ends up in our oceans.

studies show that today's top companies currently have no commitment to fully protect forests.

OUR MISSION

downroot aims to increase social responsibility of businesses through developing a societal measurment standard, raising awareness, and working together to reverse the harmful effects on the environment

What We Do

downroot has three main strategies: the pledge, fundraising initiatives, and hands-on volunteering
